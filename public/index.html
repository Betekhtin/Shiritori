Rules

- Words may not be repeated
- Only nouns are permitted
- If a word ends with small or long vowel, the next word will start with the previous kana
- A player who plays a word ending in the mora ん loses the game, as no Japanese word begins with that character

About

Shiritori (しりとり) is a Japanese word game in which the players are required to say a word which begins with the final kana of the previous word.
